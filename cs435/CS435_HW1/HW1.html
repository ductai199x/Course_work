Contrast stretching

Looking at the distribution of pixel values in the dimmed image, there are potentially 4 peaks, which situated in between: [0, 68], (68, 100], (100, 123], (123, 129]. Since the image is dark, we apply contrast stretching so that the image appear lighter and less blurry

[0, 68] →\rarr→ [0,100]

(68, 100] →\rarr→ (100,160]

(100, 123) →\rarr→ (160, 200]

(123, 129] →\rarr→ (200, 255]
